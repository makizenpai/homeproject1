How does it Work?

THE SELLING & BUYING PROCESS

  • INITIAL CONSULTATION & PLANNING

    Involves meeting with the client to discuss their property requirements, understanding their preferences, budget, and timeline, on how to effectively sell their property.

  • COMPLETE TRANSACTION PROCESS

    Finalization of paperwork, coordination, appraisals, and facilitation of the closing process, ensuring a smooth transfer of ownership between the buyer and seller.

  • DEVISE & EXECUTE MARKETING PLAN

    Involves creating and implementing a tailored strategy to showcase properties to potential buyers, maximizing visibility and generating interest in the listings.

  • AFTER - SALE SERVICE

    Providing ongoing support and assistance to clients after the property transaction, addressing any post-sale issues, and ensuring customer satisfaction with their new property.

    (NOT APPLICABLE TO BUYING)

  • REVIEW OFFERS & REACH AGREEMENT WITH BUYER

    Evaluating purchase proposals and negotiating terms with potential buyers to finalize a mutually beneficial agreement for the sale of the property.
